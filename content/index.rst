Latent Representation of PDE Solutions
=======================================
.. important::
   This tutorial demonstrates autoencoder-based representation learning on solutions of a
   convection-diffusion equation across multiple 2D grid resolutions, with cross-modal
   latent-space alignment.

  - **NAIC Portal**: https://www.naic.no/
  - **Orchestrator**: https://orchestrator.naic.no/
  - **Repository**: https://github.com/NAICNO/wp7-UC7-latent-pde-representation
